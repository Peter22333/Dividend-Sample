What is Staking?

Staking involves locking your $DIVIDENDS tokens for a predetermined period to earn enhanced dividend rewards. The longer you stake, the higher your multiplier and potential earnings.

How Earnings Are Calculated

1% of all trading volume is collected as fees. 70% of these fees are distributed proportionally to stakers based on their share of the total staked supply and chosen multiplier.

Lock Period

Once staked, your tokens are locked for the selected duration. Early withdrawal is not permitted, ensuring commitment and stable rewards for all participants.

Risk Considerations

Staking involves locking funds and potential smart contract risks. Earnings depend on trading volume and market conditions. Only stake what you can afford to lock up.
